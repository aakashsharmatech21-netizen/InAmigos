Our Story

Founded on September 23, 2020 by Govind Shukla, InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered non-profit headquartered in Chhattisgarh. We believe in the power of collective action to create lasting social change.

We hold 80G & 12A certifications, are CSR-1 registered for corporate partnerships, NITI Aayog registered, and ISO 9001:2015 certified — ensuring every rupee raised goes where it matters most.

Our Flagship Projects

Six key projects targeting India's biggest social issues - including hunger and living conditions.

🍲

Project Seva

Providing healthy food and necessary clothing to communities in need, so that no family has to sleep hungry or endure harsh weather conditions.

📚

Project Bachpanshala

Helping kids escape poverty by providing them with good education, learning opportunities, and necessary school supplies so they can have a brighter future.

🐾

Project Jeev

Helping, healing, and looking after animals that are in trouble — because kindness doesn't just stop at people.

👩‍💼

Project Udaan

Giving women the tools and skills they need to gain independence, confidence, and the ability to actively contribute to their families and society.

🌿

Project Prakriti

Tree planting events, community clean-ups, and workshops on conservation help create a better, more sustainable world for people to come.

🛠️

Project Vikas

Supporting young individuals in securing better job prospects through structured training initiatives that provide the necessary skills for meaningful and fulfilling careers..
