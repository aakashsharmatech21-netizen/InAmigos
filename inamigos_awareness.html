Who We Are

Founded on September 23, 2020 by Govind Shukla, InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered non-profit headquartered in Chhattisgarh. We believe in the power of collective action to create lasting social change.

We hold 80G & 12A certifications, are CSR-1 registered for corporate partnerships, NITI Aayog registered, and ISO 9001:2015 certified — ensuring every rupee raised goes where it matters most.

Our Flagship Projects

Six focused initiatives addressing India's most critical social challenges — from hunger to habitat.

🍲

Project Seva

Distributing nutritious food and essential clothing to underprivileged communities, ensuring no family goes to bed hungry or suffers in extreme weather.

📚

Project Bachpanshala

Breaking the cycle of poverty through quality education, learning sessions, and school supplies for children who deserve a better tomorrow.

🐾

Project Jeev

Rescuing, rehabilitating, and caring for animals in need — because compassion extends beyond human boundaries.

👩‍💼

Project Udaan

Equipping women with skills and financial tools to become independent, confident contributors to society and their families.

🌿

Project Prakriti

Tree plantation drives, clean-up campaigns, and conservation workshops to build a healthier, more sustainable planet for future generations.

🛠️

Project Vikas

Enhancing employability through structured skill development programs that give youth the tools they need to secure meaningful livelihoods.
