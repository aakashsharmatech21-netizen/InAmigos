About Us

InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla. It is headquartered in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh and is 80G & 12A certified, CSR-1 registered, ISO 9001:2015 certified and listed under NGO Darpan - NITI Aayog.

We believe in the power of working together to solve social problems. Our work focuses on food, education, women empowerment, animal welfare and environmental protection. Anyone who wants to help can become an AMIGO.

Our Ongoing Projects

🍲 Project SEVA

Providing food and clothes to poor and needy people through regular drives so no family has to sleep hungry.

📚 Project Bachpanshala

Helping children from underprivileged families receive education, books and school supplies.

👩‍💼 Project Udaan

Providing skill training to women so they can become financially independent.

🌿 Project Prakriti

Organizing tree plantation and cleanliness campaigns to protect the environment.

🐾 Project Jeev

Taking care of stray animals by feeding them and spreading awareness about animal welfare.

🛠️ Project Vikas

Helping young people gain practical skills for better career opportunities.
